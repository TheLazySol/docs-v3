# Getting Started

Welcome to Epicentral Labs! This guide will help you get started with our decentralized options trading platform on Solana.

## What is Epicentral Labs?

Epicentral Labs is a decentralized options trading platform built on Solana. We provide:

- **Decentralized Options Trading**: Trade options without intermediaries
- **Automated Market Making**: Advanced AMM for options pricing
- **Risk Management**: Built-in risk management tools
- **Liquidity Mining**: Earn rewards by providing liquidity

## Quick Start

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

First, you'll need to connect a Solana wallet to our platform:

- **Phantom**: Most popular Solana wallet
- **Solflare**: Alternative wallet option
- **Backpack**: Developer-friendly wallet

### 2. Fund Your Account

Deposit SOL or USDC to start trading:

```typescript
// Example: Check wallet balance
const balance = await connection.getBalance(wallet.publicKey);
console.log(`Balance: ${balance / LAMPORTS_PER_SOL} SOL`);
```

### 3. Start Trading

Navigate to the trading interface and:

1. Select your preferred asset
2. Choose call or put options
3. Set your strike price and expiration
4. Execute your trade
